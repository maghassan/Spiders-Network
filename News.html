The new NANIS PRESIDENT!

It's today that the new National Association Of Niger State Students (NANISS) A.B.U chapter, president was elected. The president Sulaiman Adam Sulaiman (SAY) said that he will make sure that every Niger student will sure enjoys what is meant to be enjoyed by students, which include free internet access for all, free food for all, free books for all and also good medical attention for all.
